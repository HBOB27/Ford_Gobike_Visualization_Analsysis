Preliminary Wrangling¶

This dataset is provided by Ford Gobike from June 2017 to July 2018, which is the data of the bike rides in San Francisco Bay area. The data contains info about the rider and info about the ride path.

Story behind this Analysis?¶

For sure all of us hates the traffic jam specially on rush hours, so why we dont save time, money and have some exersice while moving to work and bak to home! GoBike by Ford gives us this advantage by the bike sharing system, with thousands of public bikes for use across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose. The bike share is designed with convenience in mind; it’s a fun and affordable way to get around town.¶
So it would be interesting to analyze the GoBike data to know who we should target in the future. In this analysis i will be focusing on two things:¶
1-Who are using our bikes?¶
2-When do they use them, are there any differences in use patterns?¶
