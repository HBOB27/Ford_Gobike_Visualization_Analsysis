Investigation Overview¶

In this analysis, I want to look into the characteristics of the riders to see how can we increase the number of riders.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consists of information regarding 1,538,086 bike ride from Jun 2017 until July 2018. It contains some data that desribes the rider (date of birth, gender, and type) and others describing the ride (duration, bike ID, time, station..) Data can be found on Gobike website https://www.fordgobike.com/system-data.
